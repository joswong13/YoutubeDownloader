This is a simple youtube downloader that uses "https://en.savefrom.net" to download videos. Currently, the app only searches 
for videos and not for channels or playlists.

Screenhots:
Screenshots of the app is in the "app/Screenshots" folder.

How to use:
1) search for a video
2) click on copy url
3) paste the url into the text box on "https://en.savefrom.net" at the bottom of the screen
4) video will be downloaded to downloads folder
